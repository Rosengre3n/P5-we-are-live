Unity textures info

Names of textures provided by us contain information regarding their use and variant.
The convention is following:
 
Each folder contains two sets of textures for LOD 0 and LOD 1.
Names of textures contain information regarding their use.

Example:

baguette_01_L0_AO

reffers to:

Ambient Occlusion map for LOD 0 of baguette_01.
